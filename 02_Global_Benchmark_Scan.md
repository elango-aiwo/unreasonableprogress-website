# GLOBAL BENCHMARK SCAN
## Who does anything like this worldwide — and what we take from each
Unreasonable Progress™ · Research annexe · July 2026

**Headline finding:** Longevity-for-the-rich exists. Billionaire advisory exists. High-ticket peer networks exist. **No established firm anywhere integrates biological-age reversal AND wealth multiplication (deals, AI, partnerships, policy) as one founder-led offering.** The closest articulated concept worldwide is AIWO's own Health & Wealth Summit 2025 ("Where Longevity Meets Legacy"). The category is open.

## 2. Peer networks & billionaire advisory

- **TIGER 21** — $10M asset minimum, ~$33k/yr dues, 12–15-member confidential groups, "Portfolio Defense." → *Take: the capped small-group format; the 12-client Circle is our version.*
- **YPO / Vistage** — $4k–25k/yr leadership peer models. → *Take: cadence discipline; too mass for our tier.*
- **Family-office advisory** (Cambridge Associates OCIO ~$101B AUM; UBS/HSBC/Morgan Stanley family-office units) — bps-based, institutional, impersonal; several now bolt on "Private Health Advisory" as a **perk**. → *Take: they treat health as a concierge perk; we treat it as the foundation asset — that inversion is the category.*

## 3. High-ticket personal advisory (the fee-model benchmark)

- **Tony Robbins Platinum Partnership** — ~$85k first year (trips + access). → *Take: experience-wrapped membership.*
- **Marshall Goldsmith** — ~$250k per CEO engagement, **paid only on stakeholder-measured behaviour change**. → *Take: outcome-contingent fees build trust at this altitude; adopt for the Decade Compact success economics.*
- **Teneo** — CEO advisory retainers reportedly up to $250k/month; combines strategy, comms, risk, government affairs under one senior counselor. → *Take: the multi-domain principal-level standing retainer is proven at ~$3M/yr; our Tier 2/3 pricing sits comfortably inside precedent.*

## 4. Government relations / policy access

Brunswick, APCO, DGA/Albright Stonebridge, Teneo: senior-counselor retainer models selling *access + judgment*, rarely public fees. → *Take: (a) the pillar is legitimate and priced high when demonstrably clean; (b) rename away from "lobbying"; (c) publish red lines — the compliance posture is itself a selling point to listed-company families.*

## 5. AI transformation advisory

McKinsey QuantumBlack ($300–700/hr, 3–18-month programs), the Palantir **forward-deployed engineer** pattern now copied by BCG/McKinsey (McKinsey–Google Transformation Group, April 2026). Boutique AI advisory to family conglomerates is fragmented — no dominant brand in India. → *Take: deliver W2 with a small forward-deployed builder squad, not decks; "revenue-per-employee" as the client-legible AI metric; white space for the family-conglomerate segment in India.*

## 6. Health + wealth combined — the white space

Adjacencies only: private banks add health concierge (Morgan Stanley Private Health Advisory); family-office service firms list wellness modules; Diamandis cross-sells longevity trips to an investor community; Robbins pairs Platinum with part-ownership of Fountain Life. **Nobody makes biological age the explicit foundation of a wealth-multiplication mandate, led by one principal, measured on one scorecard.** That is the gap Unreasonable Progress occupies.

## Fee-structure patterns observed (and our stack)

1. Annual memberships $15k–100k → our Tier 1/2 base
2. Senior-counsel retainers to $250k/month → our Tier 2 anchor
3. Outcome/success fees (Goldsmith) → our Decade Compact success fee
4. Equity/carry in cohort ventures (Diamandis) → our Decade Compact carry
5. Scarce capped experiences ($70k, 60 seats) → our Immersion + Circle summit

## Five transferable design principles

1. **Founder-as-product** — Attia, Diamandis, Robbins, Goldsmith all price personal judgment, not deliverables. Siva's arc (built → sold → lost → rebuilt → reversed his own age) is the strongest founder story in the set.
2. **Scarcity is the pricing engine** — cap everything (12 clients, 60 summit seats, 5 Compacts) and enforce the cap publicly.
3. **Skin in the game** — outcome fees and equity outperform hourly billing with billionaires; they signal confidence and 10x the economics.
4. **One retainer, many domains** — bundle health + deals + AI + policy at the principal level (Teneo proves the willingness to pay).
5. **Ladder the funnel** — diagnostic entry → annual membership → ultra-tier compact; monetise the same client at 3+ price points (every comparable does).
