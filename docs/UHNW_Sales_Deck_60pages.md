# UNREASONABLE PROGRESS™
## The UHNW Sales Deck — 60 Pages, Page-Wise Content

**Audience:** H1 — Ultra High Net Worth Individuals (₹1,000 crore+ net worth; promoters and first-generation wealth creators, typically 50–70)
**Setting:** Private, in-person or 1:1 video presentation. Never emailed cold. Never left behind without the confidentiality wrapper (Page 2).
**Design language:** Matches the website — paper white / ink black, one Signal Gold accent, Archivo display type, IBM Plex Mono for every number, Newsreader italic for the founder's voice only. No stock photos, no icons, no charts with hue. Evidence in monospace; claims in display type.
**Compliance rules baked in:** every figure is a *target range* (marked †), no guarantees, no assured returns, "Policy & Sovereign Access" (never the L-word), fees disclosed verbally upon qualification, all client references anonymised.

**The persuasion arc (7 acts):**
| Act | Pages | Job |
|---|---|---|
| I — The Opening | 1–10 | Earn 60 seconds of attention; make the category feel inevitable |
| II — The Founder | 11–18 | Convert the diligence risk into the credential |
| III — Engine One: Health | 19–28 | Convert mortality fear into a measured system |
| IV — Engine Two: Wealth | 29–40 | Make 40 years of judgment legible as a system |
| V — The Practice | 41–50 | Show the machine: tiers, scorecard, rhythm, the Circle |
| VI — Evidence & Objections | 51–55 | Win the comparison; answer the unasked questions |
| VII — The Close | 56–60 | The ask, the gates, the seat |
